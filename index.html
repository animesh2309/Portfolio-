Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about my process and services.

What is your pricing structure?

Pricing is project-based and depends on the scope, complexity, and specific requirements of your website. Reach out with your project details, and I will provide a custom, transparent quote.

How long does it take to build a website?

A standard business landing page typically takes 1-2 weeks, while more complex e-commerce or custom web applications can take 3-6 weeks. I provide a strict timeline before starting.

Do you offer hosting and maintenance?

Yes, I assist with deployment and hosting setup (e.g., Vercel, Netlify, or custom servers). I also offer monthly maintenance packages to keep your site updated, secure, and fast.

Are your websites SEO optimized?

Absolutely. Every site I build follows strict technical SEO guidelines, including semantic HTML, fast loading speeds, metadata, and mobile responsiveness to ensure you rank well on Google.

Do you provide revisions?

Yes, standard projects include 2-3 rounds of revisions during the design and development phases to ensure the final product aligns perfectly with your vision.
